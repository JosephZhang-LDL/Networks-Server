The Stand - Book Review

"The Stand" is a post-apocalyptic horror/fantasy novel by Stephen King. The book follows a group of survivors after a deadly virus wipes out most of the world's population.

The story is gripping and intense, with well-developed characters and a complex plot. King's writing style is engaging and descriptive, making it easy to visualize the world he has created.

Overall, "The Stand" is a must-read for fans of horror and post-apocalyptic fiction. It's a long book, but the journey is well worth it.

Rating

4.5/5 stars
